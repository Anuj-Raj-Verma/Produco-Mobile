# ✅ Produco: The All-In-One Personal Operating System

Produco is a comprehensive, offline-first Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to manage every aspect of a student's life. From tracking rigorous academic attendance and competitive exam prep to managing college fees, daily diets, and financial transactions, Produco serves as a unified digital dashboard. 

Built with a mobile-first, Material Design aesthetic, Produco operates entirely in the browser using `LocalStorage`, ensuring maximum speed, offline capability, and absolute data privacy.

---

## 🚀 Key Highlights
* **Progressive Web App (PWA):** Fully installable on iOS, Android, and Desktop. Works 100% offline.
* **Privacy-First:** No databases, no servers. All data lives directly on your device.
* **"God Mode" Analytics:** An automated dashboard that cross-references data across different modules over a 7-day rolling window to identify personal behavioral patterns.
* **Strict Mode Productivity:** Built-in focus tools that retroactively hold you accountable if you get distracted by other apps.
* **Rich Exports:** Built-in PDF generation for financial reports, college fee receipts, and study notes using `jsPDF`.

## 📱 Feature Breakdown

### 1. 🏠 Smart Dashboard & God Mode Insights
The central hub of the app, providing a bird's-eye view of your day and deep behavioral analytics.
* **Daily Summary:** Shows a quick fraction of completed vs. pending tasks.
* **Standard Alerts:** Warns you about low attendance, pending fees, digital burnout (Screen > Study), and slipping diets.
* **Cross-Module Analytics (God Mode):** Analyzes 7-day rolling data to find hidden keystone habits. 
  * *Example:* Correlates hours of sleep with screen time spikes, or identifies how working out impacts your diet consistency.

### 2. ⏱️ Dual Focus Engine
A robust time-management module resilient to background app throttling.
* **Auto-Tracker (Stopwatch):** Tracks active hours and automatically saves the decimal duration directly to the Lifestyle module.
* **Granular Tagging:** When studying, assign specific tags (University Exams, LeetCode, Hackathon, Gov Prep) to categorize where your time actually goes.
* **Pomodoro (Countdown):** Standard 25/30/60 minute deep-work sessions with Web API Push Notifications upon completion.
* **Strict Mode (The Distraction Enforcer):** Uses retroactive timestamping to track if you leave the app while a Pomodoro is running. If you are away for more than 15 seconds, the session fails and logs a "Distracted" mark.

### 3. 🕒 Lifestyle Tracker
Designed to monitor burnout and physical health.
* **7-Day Rolling Averages:** A summary card calculates your average study, screen, and workout hours over the past week.
* **Daily Logging:** Log Wake/Sleep Times, Study Hours, Screen Time, and Workout Hours. Data is automatically fed from the Focus Engine.

### 4. 🍏 Diet Consistency
A customizable daily checklist to keep physical health on track.
* **Custom Targets:** Track Water intake, Protein, No Sugar, specific meals, etc.
* **Monthly Consistency Chart:** A dynamic `Chart.js` doughnut chart showing your overall success vs. missed targets for the month.

### 5. 🎓 Attendance Tracker
A highly granular academic tracker built for university life.
* **Semester Management:** Create, name, and switch between multiple semesters.
* **Smart Merging:** The "Today" view automatically merges consecutive identical subjects into single blocks.
* **Advanced Analytics:** Automatically calculates your precise attendance percentage, how many "safe bunks" you have left, or exactly how many classes you need to attend to reach the 75% threshold.

### 6. 🏛️ Foundation (Gov Exam Prep)
A dedicated module for competitive exam preparation.
* **Subject Progress:** Track completed chapters vs. total chapters for major subjects with visual progress bars.
* **Knowledge Vault (Notes):** Add rich text notes categorized by subject. Export all notes to a beautifully formatted PDF. If exported at the beginning of the month, the app smartly offers to wipe the digital UI to start the new month fresh.

### 7. 💸 Transactions & Finance
A clean, straightforward income and expense tracker.
* **Balance Calculation:** Instantly calculates total income, total expense, and net balance for the viewed month.
* **PDF Reports:** Download a fully formatted monthly transaction report with color-coded income/expenses.

### 8. 🏛️ College Fees
Keep track of massive university expenses without the stress.
* **Categorization:** Split fees into "Academic" (by semester) and "Mess" (by selected months/year).
* **Total Aggregation:** A dedicated card sums up your "Total Paid Overall" across all categories.
* **PDF Export:** Export your entire fee history, including Transaction IDs and Dates, to a PDF.

### 9. 📅 Calendar, Streaks & Mood Tracker
* **Calendar Agenda:** A built-in task manager and event logger tied to a monthly calendar.
* **Mood Tracker:** Log days as Good, Normal, or Bad, visualized on a monthly grid.
* **Streaks:** Build habits with customizable target days (e.g., 21, 30, 90). 

### 10. ⚙️ Data Management
* **Backup & Restore:** Export your entire `LocalStorage` database as a `.json` file to safely back up your life, and restore it on any new device.

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture

* **Frontend:** Vanilla HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+).
* **UI/UX:** Custom CSS implementing Google's Material Design 3 (MD3) guidelines, featuring dark/light mode toggles, bottom sheets, full-width edge-to-edge layouts (`viewport-fit=cover`), and dynamic CSS status indicators.
* **PWA:** Utilizes `manifest.json` and a Service Worker (`sw.js`) for aggressive offline caching and native app-like installation on mobile home screens.
* **Deployment:** Automated CI/CD pipeline using GitHub Actions to deploy to GitHub Pages with Read/Write workflow permissions.
* **Libraries Used:**
  * `Chart.js` (Data visualization).
  * `jsPDF` & `jspdf-autotable` (Tabular PDF reports).

## 📥 Installation & Setup

Because Produco is a client-side PWA, there is no backend setup required.

**To run locally:**
1. Clone the repository.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.

**To install on a mobile device:**
1. Visit the live GitHub Pages URL on your mobile device (Safari for iOS, Chrome for Android).
2. Tap **"Share" -> "Add to Home Screen"** (iOS) or **"Install App"** (Android).
3. Produco will now appear on your home screen as a native, edge-to-edge offline application!
